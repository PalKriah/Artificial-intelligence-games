About the project

This project was made for artifical intelligence course.

It contains two games:

  • The first is a one person gamewhich solves a chess problem (2.45)
  • The second is a two person game played on 3x3 board with three different colored stones. (2.25)

One person game:

The task is to get the chess pices from the first state to the secound according to the chess rules.

Two person game:

This game is played by two person on 3x3 board with red, yellow and green stones.

The players come after one another and can choose one of the following options:

  • A red stone can be placed on an empty field
  • A red stone can be changed to a yellow stone
  • A yellow stone can be changed to a green stone

The player whose step after there is a line, row or diagonal of 3 same coloured stones wins.
